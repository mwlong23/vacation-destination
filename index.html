Bali - Indonesia

About

  • Bali is an Indonesian island located in the westernmost end of the Lesser Sunda Islands.
  • Balinese or simply Bali is a Malayo-Polynesian language spoken by 3.3 million people.
  • The population in Bali is 4.225 Million people
  • Bali is located in Indonesia, and Indonesia is a predominantly Muslim country. However, surprise, surprise! The majority of the Balinese practice Hinduism.
  • Dont be too shocked if locals wish you a happy new year sometime along the year. They traditionally keep a different calendar that has its origins in the Hindu religion. It consists of 10 weeks made of 10 days.
